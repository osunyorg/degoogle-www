About the No To Big Tech Movement

This is a small, people-powered movement that was setup with a few objectives in mind:
 Raise awareness of the problems with Big Tech
 Provide a platform and community to help people leave Big Tech
 Encourage people to make the connection between Big Tech and the rise of authoritarianism (see Donald Trump and the US)
 Empower people to use their voice to make a difference - leave Big Tech, support the ethical alternatives
 Be inclusive of anyone who wants to become a part of the movement
 Be decentralised in its setup - no dictatorship, no posturing, no people platforming to raise their profile... a movement that is agile and fluid in its approach

The current team

These are the initial people involved in kicking off the movement - we hope to include and feature as many people as we can here because after all, this is a people-powered movement.
