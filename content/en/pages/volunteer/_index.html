body: >-


For the DeGoogle movement to be a success it needs your help!  We need all sorts of volunteers, from those happy to help with admin to those willing to host DeGoogle webinars.  Whatever your experience or background, I'm sure we can find a way to include you to be a part of this movement! Important note - WE DON'T EXPECT PEOPLE TO BE ABLE TO VOLUNTEER 100+ HOURS PER WEEK! Every little helps - you can drop in and drop out as and when you like (after all, we've probably all got other things to be doing too)... Just fill in the form below to register your interest and we'll come back to you with the next steps.
