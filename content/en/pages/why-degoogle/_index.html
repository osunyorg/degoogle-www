Contribute to this website

DeGoogle is a completely decentralised, people-powered grassroots movement. It relies on pro-active involvement of people who feel strongly that we shouldn't be forced into participating in this Big Tech monopoly that we have at present. That said - we actively need help updating the website. We need people who can carry out basic web research to help us document reasons to move away from Google.  If that sounds of interest, please fill out the form on our volunteer page .
