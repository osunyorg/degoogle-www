I think we are required legally to have some form of privacy policy - but in our case (to our knowledge) we aren't tracking anything about you whilst you're visiting this website.

We don't use tracking cookies (we don't like this form of web surveillance) whilst this could have meant the use of another data-harvesting Google product (eg Google Analytics). 

So - we should be cookie free and the only data we might ask for is from those people who complete the LiberaForm as a potential volunteer - in which case any personal contact information is collected solely to respond to your request. 

Of course if you sign up for our mailing list (powered by Keila) we will also be storing your email address.

Your rights as a User
As you know, we value your privacy. You may exercise your rights as a user at any time. For your information, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Rectify inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Request the deletion of your data
  • Restrict or object to its processing
  • Request data portability
  • Withdraw your consent at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with the competent data protection supervisory authority in your country of residence)

To exercise any of these rights, you may contact us at: matt@degoogle.online
