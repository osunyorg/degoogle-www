Tom Rippon is a writer who's spent more than 10 years marketing for, more often than not, Big Tech. He discovered the No To Big Tech movement while researching how to de-Google his personal and professional lives and joined the community to help guide more people towards making the change.

Tom is an advocate for independent European technology infrastructure and data sovereignty. Other causes he cares about include the environment, children's literacy and equal access to the arts.
